Concept: 'Paper Route'

Top Down View
One Screen
6 Houses With Mailboxes
7 Newspapers for you to throw

Controls
Move with Analogue Stick 1
Aim with Analogue Stick 2 
	A small indicator appears showing where you are aiming
Throw with a button
Papers keep flying indefinetely and leave the screen if they don't hit a mailbox

Flow:
If you run out of papers without delivering to all the papers the game resets with a large 'TRY AGAIN' message

If you deliver all 6 the game ends
'THE END'

It then returns to the title screen asking you to 'Press Stuff' to Begin
